Innit Crew is an organization of youth set up for solely charitable purposes. We rely on public fundraising to support our activities, the funding for a foundation typically comes from a single individual, a family or a corporation. Our journey this year to the Remar Orphanage Home, Nsawam on the 26th of December marks our 6th outreach program. Giving to the Orphans has been an encouraging experience for the past years and still counts as one of the best among all our yearly activities. Loading of food items, clothings, learning accessories and toiletries. Taking off with joy and love, our road trip experience, music and dancing in the bus with pictures and videos. Touring with the crew, seeing new places and exploring the streets of Accra to our destination has been an amazing experience these past years. This year's outreach program with the kids at Remar Orphanage Home comes with amazing activities like knowing the history about the Orphanage Home, How it was founded, Talks with the staff about how they handled the situation there during this times of Covid-19, Creative skills of the Children, Singing and Dancing, Balloon Decorations, Face Painting & Photoshoots" following Covid-19 Protocols. Our after donation hangout has been part of our yearly programs, we go to a location to eat and drink, chat, take photos and spend a little time before we make our trip back to our homes. It has been and still the awesome experience with ourselves and the kids from 2015.
