What is Innit?

The word Innit stands for positive impacts. This resulted in having the slogan It's A Push and You On Top
It's a push because we always aim to motivate one another to aim higher so you can be on top.

Our Annual Event is a charity outreach to wherever help is needed based on nominated places. Vision is to be known worldwide for the positive impact on youths and society as a whole. Core Values Respect, TeamWork, Enthusiam, Positiveness
